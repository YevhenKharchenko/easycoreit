About

Professional IT training, real-world impact.

Easy Core IT combines expert-led courses, hands-on simulations, and enterprise-grade research to prepare IT professionals for modern challenges in infrastructure, cybersecurity, and cloud management.

Courses

Learn the skills that matter.

  • IT Infrastructure Fundamentals

    Master the foundations of enterprise networks, hardware, and server environments with guided labs and real-world case studies.

  • Cybersecurity Essentials

    Learn how to identify, prevent, and respond to modern cyber threats through simulations and incident response training.

  • Cloud Management & DevOps

    Gain hands-on practice with AWS, Azure, and GCP while building continuous integration and deployment pipelines.

  • Data Governance & Compliance

    Understand regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO, and learn to design secure, compliant data systems.

  • Advanced Network Security

    Explore firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and zero-trust architectures with practical lab exercises.

  • AI for IT Operations (AIOps)

    Use machine learning tools to automate monitoring, detect anomalies, and optimize enterprise IT performance.
